Jin B&B Transportation Information

Public Transportation Options

1. Take Passenger Transport

Route suggestions:

  • To Luodong Transfer Station: Take Guoguang Passenger Transport Route 1819, 1879, or Capital Passenger Transport.
    • Driving time: About 1 hour and 30 minutes (via Snow Mountain Tunnel).
    • Ticket price: Approximately NT$140-160.
    • Boarding location:
      • Guoguang Passenger Transport: Taipei Transfer Station (near Taipei Main Station).
      • Capital Passenger Transport: Science and Technology Building Station or Taipei Main Station.
  • From Luodong Transfer Station to Jin B&B:
    • By bus: Take the Taiwan Express "Green Line 17" or other buses bound for Dongshan River Water Park.
      • Get off at: Dongshan River Water Park Station.
      • Driving time: About 10-15 minutes.
      • Ticket price: About NT$20-30.
    • By taxi: The journey takes about 10-15 minutes and costs about NT$200-300.

2. Take the Taiwan Railway

From Taipei Main Station to Luodong Station:

  • Recommended trains: "Ziqiang Express" or "Regional Express".
  • Travel time:
    • Ziqiang train: About 60-70 minutes.
    • Regional express train: About 90 minutes.
  • Fare:
    • Ziqiang Express: About NT$200.
    • Regional Express: About NT$130.

From Luodong Station to Jin B&B:

  • By bus: Take the Taiwan Express "Green Line 17" from in front of Luodong Station to Dongshan River Water Park Station.
    • Driving time: About 10-15 minutes.
    • Ticket price: About NT$20-30.
  • By taxi: The journey takes about 10-15 minutes and costs about NT$200-300.

3. Chartered Car Service

  • Route: Taipei to Jin B&B.
  • Driving time: About 1.5 hours (via Snow Mountain Tunnel).
  • Cost: Approximately NT$2,500-3,000 (depending on vehicle type and number of passengers).

4. Shuttle Service

  • The B&B provides shuttle service from Luodong Station or transfer station to the B&B.
  • Reservation required: Please contact Jin B&B in advance.
  • Fee: Can be considered as an additional service or a low fee.

5. Self Drive Option

  • Car rental service: Rent a car from the airport or city center in Taipei, drive south along National Highway No. 5, pass through the Xueshan Tunnel and then transfer to Provincial Highway 9 to Dongshan River Water Park.
  • Driving time: About 1.5 hours.
  • Parking information: Jin B&B provides a free large parking lot, suitable for families and multiple travelers.
  • Navigation information:
    • Address: Dongshan Township, Yilan County (opposite Dongshan River Water Park).
    • Landmark: Search for "Jin B&B" or "Dongshan River Water Park".

Reasons for Recommendation

  • By Passenger Transport: Most convenient, fast, and suitable for solo or small groups of travelers.
  • By Taiwan Railway: Suitable for travelers with plenty of time to enjoy the scenery along the way.
  • Chartered car or self-driving: Suitable for group travel or travelers with large amounts of luggage, offering flexibility and convenience.

If you need help arranging chartered cars or transfer services, please contact Jin B&B in advance to provide a smoother travel experience!
